Mixed-size lines should not overlap

  • 11px line: cupcakes are sweet.
  • 13px line: 14px is the body default.
  • 14px line: lorem ipsum dolor sit amet.
  • 17px line: line height pulled from fontsys_line_height.
  • 22px line: not from a 5-bucket tier table.
  • 30px line: ascent + descent from real TTF.

Each list item should have a baseline that respects its own font size; no clipping or overlap between lines.
