Floating thumbnails wrap text on the right

The float pump anchors a thumbnail box to the left margin, then subsequent paragraphs flow alongside its right edge. Each line's exclusion band shrinks by the float's outer margin extent until the line's y passes the float bottom.

Below the floated element, lines snap back to the full content width. The article renders against real font metrics, with mixed-size inline runs sharing a baseline pulled from fontsys ascent / descent rather than a tiered fallback table.

Cyrillic, Greek and emoji codepoints route through their @font-face unicode-range subset when one is declared; everything else falls through to Liberation Sans.
