Wikipedia logo, floated left.

News-article style: a small thumbnail floats to the left and the article body wraps along its right edge. Once enough prose has flowed past the float bottom, subsequent paragraphs widen back to the full content width.

The float pump fetches the image after first paint, decodes via gfx2d_image_load_mem, and the next layout pass sizes the thumbnail box to the decoded intrinsic dimensions. Line-box exclusion in flush_inline shrinks each line by the float's horizontal extent for as long as line_top falls within [float_y, float_y+float_h].
