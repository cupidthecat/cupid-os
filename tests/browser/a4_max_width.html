A4 max-width / min-width / max-height / min-height

[no clamp] paragraph fills viewport content width as usual. Wraps. One. Two. Three. Four. Five.

[max-width:400px] this div is capped at 400px wide regardless of viewport size. It must be visibly narrower than the no-clamp paragraph.
[max-width:600px; margin:0 auto] capped at 600px and centered horizontally with auto margins. Should be a centered block.
[min-width:500px] forced to at least 500px wide even if content is short.

[width:200px max-width:100px] max wins, paragraph is 100px wide.

[width:200px min-width:300px] min wins, paragraph is 300px wide.

[height:50px max-height:30px] capped to 30px tall.
[min-height:80px] short content, but block is at least 80px tall.
[max-width:50%] never wider than half the viewport.
[max-width:20em] capped at 20em (= 320px on 16px body).
